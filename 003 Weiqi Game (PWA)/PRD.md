# Go Game Project - What We're Making 🎮

Hi there! Welcome to your Go game project! This document explains what we're going to build in simple words.

## What is Go? 🤔

Go is an ancient Chinese board game played on a grid. Two people play against each other. The goal is to surround more territory than your opponent.

**Quick example:**
- Each player places black or white stones on the board
- Stones get removed when they have no space to escape
- The game ends when the board is full or no more moves are possible
- The player with more territory wins!

## What We'll Build 🛠️

### The Board (We'll make an 18x18 grid)
- Think of a checkerboard but with 18 rows and 18 columns (324 squares)
- That's the standard size for professional Go games

### Your Opponent (Computer)
- You play as BLACK stones
- The computer plays as WHITE stones
- The computer will make smart moves

### Your Controls 🎯
- Move your mouse over a spot
- Click to place your black stone
- Easy peasy!

### Game Features ✨
1. **Start Button** - Click to begin playing
2. **Your Turn** - You see which spot is green
3. **Your Move** - Click to place your black stone
4. **Computer's Turn** - Computer thinks and places white stone
5. **Score Display** - See how many stones you have and who's winning
6. **Restart Button** - Start a new game anytime

## How It Works (Super Simple Version) 🔄

1. Click "Start Game" button
2. See your board with 324 empty squares
3. Click any square to place your BLACK stone
4. Computer thinks for a few seconds
5. Computer places its WHITE stone
6. Keep taking turns until someone wins!
7. Click "New Game" to start over
